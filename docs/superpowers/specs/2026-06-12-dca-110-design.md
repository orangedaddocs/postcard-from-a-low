# DCA 110 Calculator — Design Spec

**Date:** 2026-06-12 · **Status:** Approved by Jay (verbal, in-session)

## What it is

A standalone, dependency-free HTML page (`dca-110/index.html`) in the
`orangedaddocs` repo. Companion to `power-law-catch-up/index.html` — same
cream/serif/terracotta design system, cross-linked both ways.

**Premise:** DCA 110 starts July 1, 2026. You stack an extra $110 per
day, week, or month on top of your normal DCA, and you keep going until
your scenario's glide path touches the 1.0x Power Law trendline. Four
endpoints, four answers. Thesis: **the longest sale buys the most sats.**

## Inputs

- Three preset cards: **$110/month · $110/week · $110/day**. Default: $110/week.
- Custom row: any amount ($1–$10,000, clamped) + day/week/month cadence picker.
- Everything client-side. Nothing stored, nothing sent.

## Math model (identical to power-law-catch-up page)

- Anchor: June 10, 2026, multiplier M0 = 0.4625.
- Scenarios / catch-up dates / colors:
  - Fastest (Claude bull) — Dec 31, 2027 — gold `#C7901B`
  - Base, Claude — Dec 31, 2028 — terracotta `#C0532F`
  - Base, ChatGPT — Jun 30, 2029 — plum `#7B4B94`
  - Slowest (shared bear) — Dec 31, 2030 — gray `#6B7280`
- Multiplier glide: `mult(t) = M0^(1 − f)`, `f = (t − anchor)/(catchup − anchor)`, 1.0 at/after catch-up.
- Trendline: same monthly `PL_1X` table (Jun 2026 → Jan 2031), linearly
  interpolated to daily resolution.
- Spot price on date t: `mult(t) × PL(t)`.
- Buy schedule from Jul 1, 2026: monthly = 1st of month; weekly = every
  7 days; daily = every day. Last buy on or before the catch-up date.
- Each buy: `sats += amount / price × 1e8`.
- Endpoint value: `totalSats / 1e8 × PL(catchupDate)` (multiplier is 1.0 there).

## Outputs

Four scenario cards (chart colors), each showing:
- **Dollars in** (so the bear case reads as more skin in the game, not magic)
- **Sats stacked** (with BTC shown when ≥ 0.01 BTC)
- **Value at trend** (USD on the catch-up date)
- **Multiple** (value ÷ dollars in — the punchiest number)

No fiat/cash comparison (audience rejects the premise).

## Chart

One new SVG, built from the same chart-card pattern: **cumulative sats over
time**, four lines (monthly sampling of the buy engine), each ending in a dot
at its catch-up date. Steep early, flat late — visual proof the fire-sale
months do the heavy lifting. Hover tooltip (month, sats per visible
scenario). Clicking a scenario card highlights its line and dims the others;
click again to reset.

## Copy

Orange Dad voice. Short intro (3–4 paragraphs), the tool, a "how the math
works" section, the not-advice signature, same footer as the catch-up page.

## Non-goals / constraints

- No external JS, no build step, no tracking. Google Fonts only.
- Works from `file://` and GitHub Pages.
- Mobile-first; preset cards must be thumb-tappable.
